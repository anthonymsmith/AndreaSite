Featured Book

Black Boots

A Journey of Resilience and Love

Published Jan 24, 2022 156 Pages Semi-Autobiographical
★★★★★
4.9 / 5 on Amazon

Black Boots is a story about a young woman coming of age while living with the often hidden — but always lurking — presence of a chronic illness. As Goldie navigates loss, love, and self-discovery, she travels through Europe, Florida, and the back roads of New England, encountering a vivid cast of characters who shape her along the way.

Woven throughout is the gradual and relentless presence of Multiple Sclerosis: how it shadows ordinary moments, how it changes plans, and how — against all odds — Goldie finds a way to not just survive, but to thrive. Beautifully written and deeply human, this is more than a memoir of illness. It is a portrait of the enduring power of community, love, and an unbreakable spirit.

The Author

About Andrea

Andrea M. Erickson is an author whose writing draws from the full spectrum of lived experience — joy and grief, connection and solitude, illness and resilience. Her work is grounded in honesty and animated by a deep belief in the power of story to build bridges between people.

In Black Boots: A Journey of Resilience and Love, Andrea draws on her own experience with Multiple Sclerosis to craft a narrative that is at once deeply personal and universally human. Her protagonist Goldie's journey across continents and through heartbreak mirrors the kind of courage that quiet, ordinary people demonstrate every day — without recognition or fanfare.

Writing is how I make sense of things — how I find the thread that connects loss to love, and fear to hope.

Andrea's writing is dedicated to everyone who has ever faced an invisible battle, and to the friends, partners, and communities who show up anyway — the ones who make resilience possible.

Living with Multiple Sclerosis

Andrea was diagnosed with Multiple Sclerosis, and that experience is woven into the heart of Black Boots. MS is an unpredictable, often invisible disease of the central nervous system — and Andrea has channeled her journey into a story of hope, community, and the enduring power of love.
